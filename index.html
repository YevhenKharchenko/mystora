ABOUT THE GAME

Tile Kingdom blends classic tile-matching puzzles with creative kingdom building.

You play as a trusted helper to Antonio, the Royal Architect, whose dream is to restore and expand a magnificent kingdom.

Each puzzle you solve earns resources that bring castles, chambers, and gardens to life.

TILE MATCHING & PUZZLE CHALLENGES

The core gameplay is basically Mahjong, but with some cool twists.

  • Match and combine tiles to clear the board

  • Master special mechanics like ice, grass, and gum tiles

  • Work your way through tons of levels (we spent ages designing each one)

  • Think ahead to avoid blocked paths

It gets harder as you go, but not in a frustrating way - more like a good challenge.

BRAIN TRAINING & STRATEGY

Tile Kingdom isn’t about speed — it’s about smart decisions.

  • Later puzzles require careful sequencing

  • Mistakes teach strategy rather than punish players

  • Each level encourages planning and foresight

Great for people who'd rather plan their moves instead of just tapping like crazy and hoping for the best.
